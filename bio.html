SUSHIL KUMAR MODI

He has been associated with various organizations and initiatives aimed at
promoting education healthcare and social welfare in Bihar Sushil Modi's
political career started as a student activist at Patna University.
He became the General-Secretary of Patna University Students' Union in
1973 Lalu Prasad Yadav who later came to be his biggest rival was the
President of the Union at the time.

During his tenure at ABVP he led a movement against the declaration
of Urdu as the second language of Bihar and Uttar Pradesh. Modi entered
state-level politics in Bihar and became actively involved in the party's
activities in the state.
